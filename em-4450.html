Info:

Specification

Products type : PVC/PEC/ABS/ PS Plastic card/Metal card/IC card/ID card/SIM card/CPU card
Material : PVC/ABS/PET/PS
Size : ISO CR80 Standard:3.38"L*2.125"W/ 85.5mm*54mm or on demand
Thickness : 0.8~1.2mm
Minimum QTY : 200pcs
Available crafts : 4 color offset printing, magnetic stripe, embossing number, signature panel, photo, barcode, hot-stamping gold/shiver color scratch-off, series number punch, hole punched, UV Printing etc
Chip Available : EM4100, EM4205, EM4305, EM4450, TK4100, T5567 / T5577 Compatible HID 26/37bit
Features

- Full compatible with EM4100/4102 and EM4005/4105 communication
- 128 bit laser programmed ROM (64 and 96 bit option available)
- Several options of data rate and data encoding
- Manchester 32 and 64 RF clocks per bit
- Biphase 32 and 64 RF clocks per bit
- PSK 16 RF clocks per bit (subcarrier RF/2)
- FSK2 50RF clocks per bit
- Several resonant capacitor integrated on chip 0pF, 75pF or 250pF
- 100 to 150 kHz frequency range
- On-chip rectifier and voltage limiter
- No external supply buffer capacitor needed
- -40oC to +85oC temperature range
- Very low power consumption and High performances
